## 2023 Highlights

- **Event Sponsorship:** Sponsored [**Regens Unite London**](https://www.regensunite.earth/event/regens-unite-london)’s first event that saw 50+ attendees and 15+ workshop sessions, including ‘The Ecosystem: Collaborating towards a new economic system’ by [**Rachel Kan**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/rachel-sheila-kan/), ‘From Network States to Regen CoordiNATION’ by [**Monty Merlin**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/montybryant/), and ‘The Wellbeing Protocol - Building Community Wellbeing DAOs’ by [**Mark Pascall**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/markpascall/).
- **Established Connections:** Partnered with the GreenPill UK chapter and Regens Unite London.

## 2024 Vision & Plans

- **ReFi London Events**: Kicking things off with a meetup during the upcoming [**ETH London**](https://ethglobal.com/events/london2024), they aim to lead combined ReFi London x GreenPill UK x Regens Unite events and meetups. Furthermore, recognizing London's unparalleled position as a hub of talent and innovation, in addition to core ReFi & Web3 community members, they aim to attract public, private, and third-sector leaders.
- **ReFi London Knowledge Graph & Website**: Fuelling the further development of the ReFi London Knowledge Graph and its website. This platform will serve as a comprehensive map of London's regenerative economy ecosystem and locally aligned projects.

> “The roots of ReFi London are embedded in the global ambition of ReFi DAO—a vision to cultivate a worldwide regenerative economy through a network of interconnected local communities. ReFi London aspires to be a beacon among these Nodes, rallying high-impact leaders; contributing to ReFi DAO; and leveraging London's leadership in finance, entrepreneurship, technology, and academia for positive change.” - Monty Merlin Bryant
