*“How do we turn challenges like droughts, fires, and deforestation into opportunities for sustainable regeneration?”*

### **Founding Story & Key Motivations**

ReFi Sicilia emerged from the collective efforts of a diverse group passionate about sustainable land stewardship, regenerative agriculture, and community resilience. The founding team, composed of professionals ranging from blockchain developers and crypto marketers to permaculture designers and educators, came together to address the pressing challenges facing Sicily: climate change, wildfire threats, and the degradation of traditional farming systems.

The initiative began with a focus on community events and experimental projects, such as mutual aid activities and centropic agroforestry. While early trials faced setbacks, including tree planting failures, the team used these experiences to refine their approach. The formation of a legal cultural association alongside digital DAO operations has positioned ReFi Sicilia to integrate Web3 technologies with grassroots regenerative practices, aiming to create long-term impact in their bioregion.

### **What Makes Rifai Sicilia Special**

ReFi Sicilia is deeply interwoven with its bioregion, working to revitalize local agriculture, support community-led initiatives, and address environmental crises like wildfires and drought. By focusing on olive and avocado agroforestry, reforestation, and sustainable tourism, the node creates tangible solutions that resonate with the local population.

## 2024 Highlights

### Centropic Agroforestry Projects

- **What Happened:** ReFi Sicilia secured funding to establish a greenhouse, implement irrigation systems, and conduct workshops on centropic agroforestry techniques.
- **Why It Matters:** Transitioning from monoculture olive farming to regenerative agroforestry addresses climate challenges, such as drought and soil degradation, while boosting biodiversity and local food security.

### NFT-Based Event Ticketing Platform

- **What Happened:** A co-founder developed a smart contract platform for hosting events, where tickets are minted as unique NFTs. This system was used for mutual aid events and assemblies.
- **Why It Matters:** The platform democratizes event access and introduces locals to Web3 tools, fostering broader digital inclusion and innovation in community engagement.

### Mutual Aid Events and Assemblies

- **What happened**: ReFi Sicilia organized local assemblies and events, such as fire-watch collaborations and community-driven discussions, to address shared ecological and economic challenges.
- **Why it matters:** These gatherings strengthen social networks and encourage collective action for wildfire prevention, reforestation, and sustainable farming practices.

### Bioregional Mapping Initiatives

- **What happened:** The team aggregated ecological and social data using tools like Figma and Miro to map deforestation, water scarcity, and regenerative projects in Sicily.
- **Why it matters:** Mapping enhances the capacity for sensemaking, helping the team prioritize areas for intervention while fostering collaboration among diverse stakeholders.

### Silvi App Tree Planting Pilot

- **What happened:** ReFi Sicilia piloted the use of SylviApp to plant and monitor trees, integrating blockchain-based eco-credit systems into their agroforestry methodology.
- **Why it matters:** By providing proof of tree planting and maintenance, the initiative ensures transparency and accountability, laying the groundwork for scalable agroforestry projects.

### Fire Prevention and Drought Mitigation Programs

- **What Happened:** ReFi Sicilia supported local firefighting organizations with education and resources to combat wildfires. A participatory budgeting process allocated funds to these initiatives.
- **Why It Matters:** By addressing urgent environmental threats, the node not only protects land but also engages the community in collective action.

## Vision for 2025

### Regenerative Agroforestry Expansion

- **What’s Coming:** ReFi Sicilia plans to expand its agroforestry initiatives, integrating avocado production alongside olive cultivation, and scaling its eco-credit programs.
- **Why It Matters:** Diversifying crops enhances resilience against climate change while creating economic opportunities for local farmers.

### Development of a Regenerative Tourism App

- **What’s Coming:** The node aims to launch an app connecting tourists with farm stays, volunteer opportunities, and regenerative activities across Sicily.
- **Why It Matters:** Regenerative tourism ensures that economic benefits from Sicily’s booming tourism sector flow back into local projects and communities.

### Establishment of a Land Trust

- **What’s Coming:** ReFi Sicilia envisions creating a land trust to secure agricultural land for young farmers, allowing them to steward it sustainably without ownership barriers.
- **Why It Matters:** This initiative addresses land access challenges while ensuring long-term preservation and regeneration of Sicily’s landscapes.
