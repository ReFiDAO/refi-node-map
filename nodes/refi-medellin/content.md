*“We started ReFi Medellín with a simple yet powerful vision: to bring people together through Web3 talks and regenerative finance, so they can learn, adopt, and discover how these innovative financial tools can help protect and restore the environment we all depend on.”*

### **Founding Story & Key Motivations**

ReFi Medellín started with a clear purpose: bring the local community together around Web3 conversations and regenerative finance (ReFi), all while protecting the environment. The node’s main motivation has always been to show people how these new financial models can spark ecological regeneration—and empower them to make a tangible difference.

### **What Makes ReFi Medellín Special**

ReFi Medellín stands out for its unwavering commitment to diverse, engaging activities that bring people into the Web3 world. The community embraces mutual support and mentorship, making newcomers feel welcome as they explore innovative ways to use ReFi to help the planet.

## 2024 Highlights

### **English Sessions & Blockchain Adoption**

- **What Happened:** The node organized successful English sessions where participants not only practiced the language but also gained insights into blockchain transactions, including Celo network implementation.
- **Why It Matters:** By blending language learning with blockchain education, ReFi Medellín broadened access to this technology, helping more people understand how ReFi can promote environmental regeneration.

### **Collaborations & Partnerships**

- **What Happened:** Partnerships with Salvaterra, Hashhouse, Platohedro, Alternun, and Inder played a crucial role in driving activities forward.
- **Why It Matters:** Working with diverse groups and organizations allowed ReFi Medellín to tap into broader resources and expertise, ultimately fostering greater impact within the community.

## Vision for 2025

### **Boost Regenerative Activities**

- **What’s Coming:** ReFi Medellín aims to increase hands-on projects that directly contribute to environmental regeneration.
- **Why It Matters:** Strengthening these activities will deepen the node’s positive impact, connecting people with tangible ways to protect and restore local ecosystems.

### **Classes on Blockchain & Regeneration**

- **What’s Next:** The node plans to offer more courses focused on how blockchain intersects with regenerative efforts, equipping members with the knowledge to innovate and lead.
- **Why It Matters:** By bridging technology and sustainability, these classes will cultivate a new generation of changemakers committed to both community and environmental well-being.
